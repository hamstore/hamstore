# Contributing

We are open to, and grateful for, any contributions made by the community. By contributing to Hamstore, you agree to abide by the [code of conduct](https://github.com/hamstore/hamstore/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Reporting Issues and Asking Questions

Before opening an issue, please search the [issue tracker](https://github.com/hamstore/hamstore/issues) to make sure your issue hasn't already been reported.
